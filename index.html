Multi-Asset Support

With Cashu, you can conveniently and seamlessly store multiple cryptocurrencies in a single app. Our goal is to eventually support all the top 50 coins by market cap.

Be Your Own Bank

Stay independent and 100% in control of your assets. We poll balance and historical data anonymously from public servers. No registration required.

Open Source

Cashu is open source. This means that anyone can audity the source code and rebuild the app directly from it. That is the strongest possible guarantee of transparency.

Simple Secure Design

Cashu shields the user from managing private keys. They are not stored anywhere. All digital signatures are computed on the spot, derived from the user password.
